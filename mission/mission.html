Why We Exist

Our Mission

We believe that sustainable travel starts with awareness. EcoTravel gives you the data to make every journey a conscious choice for the planet.

Why Eco Travel?

Transportation is the largest single contributor to greenhouse gas emissions in developed nations. Every journey is a choice — and small choices, made consistently by millions of people, reshape the planet.

When we choose to walk, cycle, or take transit instead of driving, we don't just reduce our personal footprint. We shift cultural norms, reduce urban congestion, and contribute to a liveable future for generations after us.

The Problem

171g of CO₂ emitted per kilometer by the average car

A single round-trip drive of 20 km produces the same emissions as charging a smartphone more than 1,500 times. When public transit, cycling, or walking can serve the same trip, the environmental case is overwhelming.

The challenge isn't a lack of alternatives — it's a lack of visibility into the true cost of each choice. Most people have no idea how much CO₂ their commute produces, and that invisibility makes change harder.
